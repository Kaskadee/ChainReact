How to play?
------------

1. This game is for 2 to 4 players

2. Left-click on a wabe 
===> activates the field and sets the player color

3. You can only set in empty or your own wabes

4. Each player has one turn per round

5. If all activatable fields (Lamps) in one wabe are active the wabe 
explodes and gives each surrounding field one color

6. You win if you occupy 25 wabes 
--or--
if you take all wabes from the other players
